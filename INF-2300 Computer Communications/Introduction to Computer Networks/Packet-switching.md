Hosts break application-layer messages into packets.

## Store and Forward
- Packet transmission delay: takes L/R seconds to transmit L-bit packet into link at R bps
- Store and Forward: entire packet must arrive at router before it can be transmitted on next link

## Queuing 
Occurs when work arrives faster than it can be serviced.

### Packet queuing and loss
If arrival rate (in bps) to link exceeds transmission rate (bps) of link for some period of time
- Packets will queue, waiting to be transmitted on output link
