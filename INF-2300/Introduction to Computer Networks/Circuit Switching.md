End-end resources allocated to, reserved for "call" between source and destination
- In a diagram, each link has four circuits
	- Call get 2nd circuit in top link and 1st circuit in right link
- Dedicated resources: no sharing
	- Circuit like performance
- Circuit segment is idle if not used by call

## FDM and TDM
### Frequency Division Multiplexing (FDM)
- Total bandwidth available is divided into a series of non-overlapping frequency bands, where each uses their own separate signal.
### Time Division Multiplexing (TDM)
- Time divided into slots
